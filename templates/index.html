♠ About us ♠


Welcome to Ubuntu group, here we have professional hackers working remotely and around the clock to help you recover your lost assets.


We work globally and have been doing this since 2017. We have recovered funds for victims in different parts of the world. US, Uk, Australia, Canada, Sweden, etc.


We are happy you are here and can't wait to help you recoup your lost funds. Ubuntu Group is a recovery organization founded in 2017 by a group of anonymous hackers dedicated to helping scam victims get justice. We have done hundreds of recoveries over the years and impacted the lives of people positively.


Over the years, we have had a great number of successes and a few failures. Our recent statistics show that we have had a 93% success rate in all our recoveries, with only a 7% failure rate. For those we failed to help, we made donations to them from our donation box donated by victims of scam after a successful recovery.
